About

Martin started his trade career as a plasterers apprentice before owniung his own building business with a partner through 1980s. Over the years Martin has found a niche in providing turnkey solutions for small to mid size domestic contracts. He uses a small selection of trusted contractors and suppliers to ensure he has control of all aspects of the planning, design and build phases. Martin now lives with his wife, Lynda, in Bawtry... close to family and friends.
